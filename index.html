about us

D&B Engineers and Construction is a construction company that specializes in undertaking and delivering turnkey projects and part works like interiors, UPVC windows, renovation works, waterproofing, ant-termite solutions, painting, etc.

Founded in 2021 by two childhood friends: Dinesh Kumar and Bala Murugan, D&B and the core ideas that it is built upon were conceived back in 2016 with the central motto to ‘Satisfy our Beloved Clients through Quality Construction and Best Services’.

Dinesh has multiple years of experience working in a Chennai-based architecture firm before he moved to Mysore and began working there for a while.

Bala began his career in a construction company based in Chennai before moving to Bahrain and working there for several years across multiple projects.

The duo decided to quit their respective jobs and start this company with the hopes of realizing and executing their core motto that they set for themselves five years back.

What do we do?

A lot of people feel confused and overwhelmed when they begin to construct their homes. They face numerous questions when they start planning:

  • Should we construct it ourselves?
  • Should we employ contractual masons?
  • Should we rely on builders?
  • Which builder would be the best choice for our project?
  • Will they use good quality materials during construction?
  • Will they be technically sound?

With us, you can put all these questions aside and be in peace.

We help significantly reduce your burden by being the complete solution that you can rely on. We take full responsibility for your home without any compromise in quality, enabling you to fulfil your dreams.

We always push our limits to provide best quality services to our clients through our unique expertise and aspire to help you realize your dreams no matter how big they might be.
